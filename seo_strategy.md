# SEO Strategy

## In scope
- Public web pages served by the Expo artifact deployment
- Guest-accessible routes in the Expo web app
- Any crawl-control files or HTML shells that affect search or social sharing

## Out of scope
- Authenticated customer dashboards and account-only routes
- Admin pages and technician-only pages
- API JSON endpoints under `/api/**`
- Native mobile-app store optimization (ASO)

## Target audience
- Prospective and existing K&S Solar Energy customers in Pakistan looking for solar installation, panel washing, warranty lookup, inverter status help, or support.

## Primary keywords
- K&S Solar Energy
- solar panel washing Pakistan
- solar installation Pakistan
- solar complaint support
- warranty search
